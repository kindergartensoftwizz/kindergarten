What We Offer

Daycare


We provide a safe, stimulating and caring environment that promotes learning through play, using a wide range of resources, and the opportunity for children to develop their social skills as they play alongside and with other children.We have our own A.C. transport facility for the comfort of students along with a lady attendant.



Summer Camp


You are always welcome to have tour of our school and see what we offer.We have our own large range of play equipments which we keep regularly updated.Children join us to have fun and to learn, through play method, the individual skills needed to enter school confidently and enthusiastically.



Infant Care


KIDS CASTLE School, which comprises bright, spacious, air conditioned rooms. We have large classrooms and an adjacent activity rooms as well as special toilet facilities with low height seats and separate washrooms for boys and girls, a kitchen for preparation of hygienic & healthy food for the children and two easily accessed outside areas.
